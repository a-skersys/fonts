A Font with Serifs Pro

Handcrafted Serif for Editorial and Modern Design

A Font with Serifs Pro delivers a distinct handwritten serif personality. First created with a blue pen and quietly embraced on book covers and editorial projects, this revised version maintains its authentic, textured appeal. The letterforms remain intentionally imperfect—lines shift, symmetry wavers, and visible irregularities serve as a reminder of its analog beginnings instead of digital uniformity. These details lend a vintage sensibility, yet A Font with Serifs Pro adapts effortlessly to contemporary design workflows.

Designed to support professional typography, the updated release brings expanded typographic function without losing its natural warmth. Carefully honed for legibility and visual texture, it offers a compelling alternative for creative direction where an expressive typographic voice is called for. Use it confidently for cover design, editorial spreads, branding, or any environment seeking type with genuine presence and character.
